Slow Cooked Beef

Type of Beef: Barbacoa

To make this juicy delight, follow the steps below:

  1. Cut beef into cubes
  2. Put in pot and add water until it covers the meat
  3. Add (salt,pepper,cumin,garlic,maggi,dried peppers,cinnamon stick,bay leaves,)
  4. Let it simmer for 5 minutes
  5. Cover the top of the pot with aluminum foil and close it with the lid (so the steam doesnâ€™t escape)
  6. Put the pot in a 150 celsius oven
  7. Let it cook for 4-6 hours

And the last step: Enjoy!
